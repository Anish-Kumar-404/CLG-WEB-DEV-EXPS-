OnePlus 11 5G

This is a detailed review of the OnePlus 11 5G.

The OnePlus 11 5G was released earlier this year, in February 2023, and it's been one of the most popular Android smartphones ever since. In fact, eight months down, despite all the great options that I have on the table, including the latest iPhones, Pixels, foldable phones etc., my current daily driver is the OnePlus 11. You see, the best part of this phone is that it offers a flagship-grade experience at a very competitive price, and it's not just me, critics as well as consumers have long praised the OnePlus 11 for its powerful performance, excellent battery life, and great camera system since its release.

OnePlus 11 5G Specifications

  • Display 6.70-inch, 1440x3216 pixels
  • Processor Snapdragon 8 Gen 2
  • RAM 12GB
  • Storage 256GB
  • Battery Capacity 5000mAh
  • Rear Camera 50MP + 48MP + 32MP
  • Front Camera 16MP
