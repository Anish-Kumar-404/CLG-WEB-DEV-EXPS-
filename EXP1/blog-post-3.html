OnePlus Nord 3 5G

This is a detailed review of the OnePlus Nord 3 5G.

OnePlus Nord 3 5G mobile was launched in July 2023. The phone comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.74-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 1240x2772 pixels. The display sports Asahi DragonTrail for protection. OnePlus Nord 3 5G is powered by an octa-core MediaTek Dimensity 9000 processor. It comes with 8GB, 16GB of RAM. The OnePlus Nord 3 5G runs Android and is powered by a 5000mAh battery. The OnePlus Nord 3 5G supports proprietary fast charging.

OnePlus Nord 3 5G Specifications

  • Display 6.74-inch, 1240x2772 pixels
  • Processor MediaTek Dimensity 9000
  • RAM 16GB
  • Storage 256GB
  • Battery Capacity 5000mAh
  • Rear Camera 50MP + 8MP + 2MP
  • Front Camera Unspecified
